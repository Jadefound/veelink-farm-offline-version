Everything your farm needs

From animal records to profit reports, VeeLink Farm covers the full farm workflow.

Herd management

Track every animal with ID, breed, age, weight, status, and value. Search and filter your herd in seconds.

Health records

Log treatments, vaccinations, and visits. Keep your animals healthy and your compliance simple.

Finances

Record income and expenses. See profit, balance, and trends at a glance.

Reports & analytics

Visual charts for income, expenses, species breakdown, and herd health. Export and share when you need them.

Mobile-first

Built for life in the field. Record data on your phone, review it on your tablet.

Dark mode

Easy on the eyes at night or in the barn. Switch between light and dark themes instantly.
